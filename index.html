FAQ

Why did you decide to become a web developer?

I've always enjoyed creating things, and love the marriage of aesthetics and functionaliy. There are endless possibilities when creating sites, and it's exciting to help a dream website come to life.

Will I get a say in the design?

Absolutely! You are the most important part of your site, and it should reflect your tastes, branding, and message.

Who are your clients?

I enjoy working with artists and makers to help them make their online presence as vibrant as their products. Small business is alive and well on the internet, but often times, the website doesn't match the style or personality of the creator.

Does the look of my site really matter?

Yes! You can have the best copy in the world, and incredible products, but you only get one chance to make a first impression, and the look is what makes it. We make snap judgements within seconds, and it's important for your brand to shine in those first few moments, as well as give people a solid feel for your personality and branding.
